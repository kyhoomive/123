Shao-Yun Fang (S’11–M’13) is currently an Associate Professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST), Taipei. Her current research interests include physical designs and designs for manufacturability. Dr. Fang was a recipient of the First Place Winner of the 2012 ACM/SIGDA Student Research Competition (Graduate Student Category), a Silver Award Winner of the 2012 TSMC Outstanding Student Research Award (Category I: Circuit Design Technologies), the Best Paper Award from the 2016 International Conference on Computer Design, the Best Paper Award from the 2016 International Symposium on VLSI Design, Automation, and Test, and the two Best Paper Nominations from the 2012 and 2013 International Symposiums on Physical Design.
